To configure the shortcuts, click the button in the upper right corner and then click Manage Extension Shortcuts.

Duplicate tab shortcut: error.

Duplicate to a new window shortcut: error.

Pop tab out to a new window shortcut: error.

New tab to the right shortcut: error.

Pop tab out to a new private window shortcut: error.

You need to allow the addon to "Run in Private Windows" for this to work (in the Details section)!!

This does not preserve the tab history!

If the window is already private then it creates a normal window.

Tip: You can perform the actions with multiple tabs highlighted.
